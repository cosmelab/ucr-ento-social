Welcome to Our Community Hub

We are the Social Committee for the UC Riverside Department of Entomology. Our committee coordinates volunteer contributions, manages event logistics, and works together to create meaningful social opportunities for faculty, students, postdocs, and staff within our department.

Our Timeline

Launch first events based on your feedback

Phase 3: Winter

Expand website with calendar, photos, and event sign-ups

Why Your Voice Matters

Every successful event starts with understanding what our community needs. Your feedback directly shapes the activities, timing, and format of department gatherings.
