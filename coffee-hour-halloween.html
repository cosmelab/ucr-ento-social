About This Event

Come enjoy coffee, treats, and good company as we celebrate Halloween together! This is our very first Social Committee event, and we're kicking things off with a spooky celebration. Whether you dress up in a costume or come as you are, all are welcome to join us for some frightfully fun festivities.

This coffee hour is a great opportunity to:

  • Meet and connect with colleagues from across the department
  • Take a break from research and enjoy some seasonal treats
  • Show off your Halloween spirit with costumes (spooky, silly, or creative!)
  • Support the Social Committee's mission to build community
